Service details

Dive deeper into what each of our core services includes and how we deliver them.

Manned Guarding

Our uniformed, SIA-licensed guards are deployed with site-specific Standard Operating Procedures. Every guard undergoes background checks, site induction, and regular performance reviews.

Services include reception security, internal and perimeter patrols, visitor management, and emergency response coordination. We provide daily logs and incident reports so your management team stays fully informed.

  • 24/7 or shift-based deployment
  • Uniformed, badged, and background-checked
  • Daily digital patrol logs
  • Incident escalation matrix

CCTV Monitoring

Our remote monitoring center operates 24/7, watching your cameras in real time. When an alert triggers, our operators verify, escalate, and dispatch within minutes.

We support integration with existing camera systems, provide cloud-based event playback, and maintain compliance-ready archives for regulatory requirements.

  • Real-time live monitoring
  • AI-assisted motion detection
  • Cloud playback and evidence storage
  • Monthly analytics reports

Access Control

We manage entry points for your staff, contractors, and deliveries with ID verification, badge systems, and visitor logs. Reduce unauthorized access and keep your facility secure.

Our systems integrate biometric scanners, smart card readers, and QR-based visitor passes for a seamless yet secure entry experience.

  • Biometric and card-based systems
  • QR visitor pass integration
  • Zone-based access restrictions
  • Real-time entry/exit logs

Rapid Response

Our mobile response teams are deployed within minutes when alarms trigger. We verify the alarm, assess the situation on-site, and coordinate with local authorities when needed.

Available 24/7, our response vehicles cover the entire service area and maintain an average response time of under 10 minutes.

  • Under 10-minute average response
  • GPS-tracked response vehicles
  • Alarm verification protocol
  • Police and fire coordination
